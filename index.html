About the Show

The TV show Bones is loosely based on books written by Kathy Reichs. The show started September 13, 2005 and ended March 28, 2017. It lasted 12 seasons. The show was follows forensic anthropologist Dr. Temperance "Bones" Brennan and cocky F.B.I. Special Agent Seeley Booth as they work together to investigate murders. Sometimes not having a lot to work with either.
